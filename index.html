How to Play

Goal

Move your piece from its home color to the opposite location point (the center cell marked with a pin).

If you are RED escape from YELLOW

If you are YELLOW escape from RED

If you are GREEN escape from BLUE

If you are BLUE escape from GREEN

Turns & Movement

  • Roll: Click Roll Dice on your turn.
  • Unlock: Your first six only unlocks your piece onto the board (no wall that turn).
  • Move: After rolling, click a highlighted cell to move exactly that many steps along connected cells.

Walls

  • When: Walls can be placed only on a turn where a six is rolled (except the first six, which only unlocks).
  • Choice: Placing a wall is optional for both you and bots.
  • Limits: Each color has 3 walls. A wall lasts for 3 of that color's turns.
  • Effect: Only the wall owner can enter its cell; others are blocked.
  • No-Place Zones: Walls cannot be placed on any location/goal cell.

Special Rule

  • Trapped on 4: If a piece is sandwiched by two walls with a one-cell gap and the roll is 4, that piece automatically returns home.

Winning (1v3)

  • Ends When: The game ends when you finish (shows rank: 1st/2nd/3rd) or all three bots finish first (You lose).
  • Ongoing: If a bot finishes before you, the game continues until you finish or all three bots finish.

Controls

  • Roll Dice: Start your turn.
  • Move: Click a highlighted target cell.
  • Place Wall: After rolling a six (not the first six), click Place Wall, then a cell to place it.
